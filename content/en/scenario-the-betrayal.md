# Scenario: The Betrayal

*"The apartment is quiet. Dawn filters through the curtains. On the table are the remains of a dinner: empty bottles, dirty dishes, overturned glasses. On the floor, near the couch, there's a dark stain. It could be red wine. Or maybe not."*

## Quick Setup

**Location:** An apartment the characters vaguely recognize · **Atmosphere:** Violated intimacy, remnants of an evening gone wrong, merciless dawn light · **Players:** 2–4 friends bound by a secret no one wants to remember · **The Question:** *Who did you betray? And what did you do after?*

*Note: This scenario does not use The Incident's Fragment Cards. The Memory Keeper uses the Suggested Fragments below or improvises at the table. Alternatively, The Incident's cards can be adapted by swapping the clinical setting for the apartment.*

## The Truth Beneath

*(All players should read this section. Your characters don't know any of this — but you do. Let this knowledge haunt your choices.)*

The apartment belongs to Marco Santini, the Catalyst's best friend. That evening Marco had organized a dinner to announce his upcoming wedding to Sofia. But Sofia and the Catalyst had been having a secret affair for months. During the dinner, drunk, they kissed in the hallway. Marco saw them.

What happened next: the Protector tried to mediate but made things worse. The Witness saw everything but didn't intervene. In the scuffle that followed, Marco fell and hit his head on the corner of the table. The stain on the floor is blood.

Marco died that night. It's unclear whether it was an accident or whether someone pushed him. Everyone decided to hide the body in the trunk of his car and pretend Marco had disappeared. Sofia fled with the car and stopped picking up her phone.

## Key NPCs

**Marco Santini** — 28. Optimistic, loyal, betrayed. The victim. He'd bought a ring for Sofia that morning. He never appears directly: he's an absence that weighs on every scene, an empty place at the table, a name no one says.

**Sofia Marchetti** — 26. Complex, scared, on the run. The players remember her in contradictory ways: someone sees her crying, someone sees her calm and calculating. Where is she now? What will she do with what she knows?

**The Neighbor** — 60. A chronic insomniac. He heard the screaming. He hasn't called the police yet, but he will soon. The players catch glimpses of him through the window, awake, watching them.

## Phase-by-Phase Guide

**Cycles 1–2 (Fog).** The apartment is your field of exploration. Fragments are sensory: the taste of wine turning bitter, the scent of an elegant outfit, laughter fading into something shrill. Every room holds a clue: broken glasses, a ring in a drawer, scratches on the wall.

Questions to ask: *Why does the wine have this aftertaste? Whose perfume is on your clothes? There's one extra glass — who was it for?*

**Cycles 3–4 (Connections).** Memories become concrete and painful. The kiss in the hallway. Marco's eyes — disbelieving, hurt, then furious. A voice screaming *"He was my best friend!"*. A body on the floor. The weight of something being lifted.

Questions to ask: *Who among you knew about the affair? Who tried to stop the fight? What did you put in the trunk?*

**Cycle 5 (Revelations).** The truth is brutal and personal. Each character discovers their specific role in the betrayal and the cover-up. The police will find Marco's car. The neighbor heard everything. Time is running out.

Questions to ask: *Can you still protect each other? Who among you calls the police? What do you say to Sofia, if you find her?*

## Suggested Fragments

The Memory Keeper uses these as narrative triggers or improvised cards during the game.

**Fog:** The taste of wine turning bitter in your mouth · Laughter transforming into something shrill · Something breaking — glass? Or bone? · The scent of an elegant outfit that isn't yours · Sudden silence after chaos · Hands trembling while dialing a number

**Connections:** A stolen kiss in the shadow of the hallway · Marco staring with disbelieving eyes · *"He was my best friend!"* · A motionless body on the floor, head at an odd angle · Four people lifting something heavy · The inside of a car trunk, at night

**Revelations:** You and Sofia had been seeing each other secretly for months · Marco fell during the scuffle — or did you push him? · All of you decided to hide the body · Sofia ran and isn't answering · The police found Marco's car at the port · One of you knew exactly what to do — as if you'd done it before
