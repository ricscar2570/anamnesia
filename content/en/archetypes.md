# The Archetypes

Choose your Archetype from the heart, not for tactical advantage. Each one represents a different way of facing trauma and contributes to the group with unique strengths and vulnerabilities. Duplicates are allowed.

| Archetype | Base Pool | Identity |
|:----------|:----------|:---------|
| The Survivor | 5 | "I'm alive when others are not." |
| The Witness | 5 | "I saw everything. But my mind shuts down." |
| The Protector | 4 | "It was my responsibility. I failed." |
| The Catalyst | 4 | "It all started because of me." |

> **Quick reminder:** 5–6 = Clear · 3–4 = Confused · 1–2 = Traumatic (+1 Echo) | **Golden Rule:** the player to your right interprets your roll.
