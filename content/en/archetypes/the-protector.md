# The Protector

> *"It was my responsibility to keep you safe. I failed."*

**Base Pool: 4 dice**

You were responsible for everyone's safety. In the crucial moment, you failed — spectacularly. Your hands tremble every time you try to help. But you can't stop trying.

## Approaches

- **Take Command** (free) — Organize, command, make the hard decisions for the group.
- **Calculated Sacrifice** (+1) — Put yourself in danger to save others, absorb the damage.
- **Weight of Command** (+2) — Remember all the times you failed. Draw from that painful experience.

## Ability: Living Shield

When another player gets a Memory Collapse (0 successes), you can sacrifice up to 3 Fragments to transfer to them. For every 2 Fragments sacrificed, you gain 1 Echo (rounded down). You must narrate how you take on the responsibility for their pain.

## Vulnerability

Others in danger without being able to help (recover 1 Fragment and −1 Stress if roleplayed. Max once per cycle).

**Key Question:** *"What threat am I ignoring and who can't I protect right now?"*
