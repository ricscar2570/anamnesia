# The Survivor

> *"I'm alive when others are not."*

**Base Pool: 5 dice**

You survived when others didn't make it. Your reflexes are sharp, but every escape makes you feel like a coward. Why you and not them?

## Approaches

- **Escape Instinct** (free) — Sense danger, spot exits, read the warning signs.
- **Protective Paranoia** (+1) — Anticipate betrayal, prepare for the worst before it happens.
- **Memory of Pain** (+2) — Remember how you survived before. Pain as a guide.

## Ability: Survival Reflexes

When you avoid a physical danger, you can reroll all dice showing 1–2. If they come up 1–2 again, you must describe who you failed to save.

## Vulnerability

Feeling like a burden to others (recover 1 Fragment and −1 Stress if roleplayed. Max once per cycle).

**Key Question:** *"Who am I failing by still being alive?"*
