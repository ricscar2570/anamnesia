# The Witness

> *"I saw everything. But every time I try to remember, my mind shuts down."*

**Base Pool: 5 dice**

You witnessed every detail of the horror. Your eyes captured every nuance. But now those memories are fragmented like glass that cuts.

## Approaches

- **Detached Observation** (free) — Notice details, read emotions, stand apart while you analyze.
- **Empathic Connection** (+1) — Feel what others feel, sense lies from the pain in their eyes.
- **Complete Vision** (+2) — See the full picture, connect all the pieces in a moment of terrible clarity.

## Ability: Collective Memory

By observing another player's roll, you can spend 1 Fragment and gain 1 Echo to "record" the results. Later in the cycle, anyone can use those recorded results instead of rolling. Maximum 2 recordings per cycle.

## Vulnerability

Being forced into immediate decisions (recover 1 Fragment and −1 Stress if roleplayed. Max once per cycle).

**Key Question:** *"What crucial detail did I notice but am too afraid to reveal?"*
