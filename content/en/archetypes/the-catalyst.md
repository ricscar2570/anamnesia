# The Catalyst

> *"It all started because of me."*

**Base Pool: 4 dice**

You set off the chain of events. Not with malice, but with a choice, a word, a gesture. And everything collapsed. Now every action you take carries the echo of that first, fatal decision.

## Approaches

- **Social Invisibility** (free) — Stay in the background, avoid attention, observe without being seen.
- **Subtle Manipulation** (+1) — Influence the group's dynamics without being discovered.
- **Pivotal Moment** (+2) — Remember the exact moment when everything changed.

## Ability: Domino Effect

When your roll results in a Memory Collapse (0 successes), any other involved players gain +1 Fragment (for this cycle only). You gain 1 Echo. Your actions always have a chain reaction.

## Special Ability: Burden of Guilt

Once per game, the Catalyst can confess their responsibility to another character. Gain 3 Fragments immediately. This does not cause cascading Stress.

## Vulnerability

Being pushed to lead or choose (recover 1 Fragment and −1 Stress if roleplayed. Max once per cycle).

**Key Question:** *"Which of my actions could make everything collapse again?"*
